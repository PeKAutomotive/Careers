# Careers at PeK Automotive

## Firmware Developer

📍 Location: Logatec, Slovenia  
💼 Type: Full-time  
🌍 Relocation: Assistance available
On-site collaboration with the software, mechanical design and manufacturing team.

PeK Automotive d.o.o. is a fast-growing company based in Slovenia. We develop advanced automotive and embedded solutions and are looking for talented Firmware Developers to join our team.

At Pek Automotive in Slovenia we design and build advanced autonomous electric systems and robotic vehicles used in demanding real-world environments such as agriculture and rescue operations.
We are expanding our R&D team and looking for an Embedded Firmware Developer who enjoy working close to hardware and solving complex engineering challenges.
You will develop firmware for electronic control units powering next-generation autonomous machines.

### What you will work on:
    • Developing embedded firmware for microcontrollers (STM32),
    • Debugging embedded firmware using oscilloscopes and measurement equipment,
    • Collaborating with hardware engineers to integrate firmware with custom electronics,
    • Creating technical documentation for firmware architecture 
### Tech stack:
    • C,
    • STM32 microcontrollers,
    • ARM Cortex-M,
    • UART, SPI, I2C, RS485, CAN
    • Git version control,
    • Embedded debugging tools.
      
### What we are looking for:
    • Experience developing embedded firmware for microcontrollers,
    • Experience with STM32 or other ARM Cortex-M platforms,
    • Good understanding of hardware communication interfaces (CAN, SPI, I2C, UART),
    • Ability to debug hardware/software interactions,
    • Experience with oscilloscopes or measurement tools is a strong advantage.
      
### Nice to have:
    • signal processing knowledge,
    • knowledge of the control system theory and experience in building real-time control systems
    
      
### What we offer:
    • Salary: €3,800 – €4,700 gross/month depending on experience,
    • Work on real-world autonomous machines and robotics,
    • Relocation support and visa assistance for Slovenia,
    • A collaborative international engineering team,
    • Career growth and learning opportunities,
    • Company cafeteria with warm lunch provided daily.
